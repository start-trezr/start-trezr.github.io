Welcome to Trezor.io Start Guide

Trezor is a hardware wallet providing advanced security for handling Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies private keys. It was created by SatoshiLabs and it is one of the most secure ways to store your digital assets.

Getting Started

To start using your Trezor device, follow these steps:

  1. Unbox your Trezor: Inside the box, you will find your Trezor device, a USB cable, a getting started guide, and recovery seed cards.
  2. Connect to your computer: Use the USB cable to connect your Trezor to your computer.
  3. Go to Trezor.io/start: Open your web browser and navigate to trezor.io/start. Follow the on-screen instructions to download the Trezor Bridge, which facilitates communication between your Trezor device and your computer.
  4. Install firmware: If your Trezor device does not have firmware installed, the website will guide you through the process of installing the latest firmware.
  5. Set up your device: Follow the on-screen instructions to name your device and create a PIN code. Make sure to write down your recovery seed on the provided cards and store it in a safe place.
  6. Access Trezor Wallet: Once your device is set up, you can access the Trezor Wallet interface through wallet.trezor.io.

By following these steps, you can ensure that your cryptocurrencies are stored securely. Remember to keep your recovery seed safe and never share it with anyone.
